GeoNor - linking Georgia and Norway

GeoNor - კავშირი საქართველოსა და ნორვეგიას შორის


GeoNor – Linking Georgia & Norway is a non-governmental and non-profit organization. The aim is to encourage links between Georgia and Norway through educational, social and cultural activities. GeoNor supports civil society development processes in Georgia and considers experience sharing as significant aspects for development
